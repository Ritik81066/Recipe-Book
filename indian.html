Spring Rolls

Ingredients

Spring Roll Wrappers: 10-12
Cabbage (shredded): 1 cup
Carrots (julienned): ½ cup
Capsicum (julienned): ½ cup
Soy sauce: 1 tablespoon
Salt and pepper to taste
Oil for deep frying

Recipe

1. Prepare Filling: Stir-fry veggies in a pan with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
2. Wrap: Place the filling in the center of each wrapper and roll tightly.
3. Fry: Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown.
4. Serve: Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

Samosa

Ingredients

Potatoes (boiled and mashed): 3
Peas: ½ cup
Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
Samosa dough (flour, water, salt): As required
Oil for deep frying

Recipe

1. Prepare Filling: Mix mashed potatoes, peas, cumin, garam masala, salt.
2. Shape Samosas: Roll dough into cones, stuff with filling, and seal edges.
3. Fry: Deep fry in hot oil until crispy and golden.
4. Serve: Serve hot with chutney.

Chicken Pakora

Ingredients

Chicken (boneless, cubed): 250g
Chickpea flour: ½ cup
Spices (turmeric, chili powder, garam masala): To taste
Water: As needed for batter
Oil for deep frying

Recipe

1. Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with spices and set aside.
2. Prepare Batter: Mix chickpea flour with water to make a thick batter.
3. Fry Pakoras: Dip chicken in batter and deep fry until crispy.
4. Serve: Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

Paneer (cubed): 250g
Yogurt: ½ cup
Spices (turmeric, cumin, garam masala, chili powder): To taste
Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
Oil for grilling
Vegetables (capsicum, onion, tomato): As needed

Recipe

1. Marinate Paneer: Mix yogurt, spices, and lemon juice. Marinate paneer and veggies for 30 minutes.
2. Grill Tikka: Skewer marinated paneer and veggies, grill until golden.
3. Serve: Serve hot with mint chutney.

Shahee Paneer

Ingredients

Paneer (cubed): 250g
Butter: 2 tablespoons
Tomatoes (pureed): 3
Cream: ¼ cup
Spices (turmeric, garam masala): To taste

Recipe

1. Make Gravy: Sauté tomatoes and spices in butter, then blend.
2. Add Paneer: Add paneer cubes to the gravy and cook.
3. Finish: Stir in cream and cook for a few more minutes.
4. Serve: Serve hot with naan or rice.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken (boneless, cubed): 300g
Butter: 3 tablespoons
Tomatoes (pureed): 4
Cream: ½ cup
Garam masala: 1 teaspoon

Recipe

1. Marinate Chicken: Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt.
2. Cook Chicken: Fry marinated chicken in butter.
3. Make Gravy: Cook tomato puree and spices, add cream and butter.
4. Serve: Mix chicken with gravy, garnish with cream, and serve hot.

Mutton Biryani

Ingredients

Mutton: 500g
Basmati Rice: 2 cups
Onions (fried): 2
Biryani spices (cloves, cinnamon, bay leaves): To taste
Yogurt: ½ cup
Oil: 3 tablespoons

Recipe

1. Marinate Mutton: Marinate mutton with yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
2. Cook Mutton: Fry onions, add marinated mutton, and cook until tender.
3. Cook Rice: Parboil basmati rice with whole spices.
4. Layer and Cook: Layer mutton and rice in a pot, cook on low heat.
5. Serve: Garnish with fried onions and serve hot.

Dal Makhani

Ingredients

Whole black lentils: 1 cup
Kidney beans: ¼ cup
Butter: 2 tbsp
Cream: ¼ cup
Spices (turmeric, cumin, chili powder, garam masala): To taste
Tomato puree: 1 cup

Recipe

1. Cook Lentils: Soak and cook lentils and kidney beans until soft.
2. Prepare Gravy: Fry spices in butter, add tomato puree, and cook.
3. Combine: Add cooked lentils, simmer with cream.
4. Serve: Garnish with butter and serve with naan or rice.

Kheer

Ingredients

Basmati rice: ¼ cup
Milk: 1 liter
Sugar: ½ cup
Cardamom powder: ½ tsp
Saffron strands: Few
Dry fruits (almonds, cashews, raisins): To garnish

Recipe

1. Cook Rice: Wash rice, cook in milk on low heat until soft.
2. Add Sugar: Stir in sugar, cardamom, and saffron, cook until thickened.
3. Garnish: Add dry fruits, serve warm or chilled.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

Khoya (grated): 200g
All-purpose flour: 2 tablespoons
Sugar: 2 cups
Cardamom powder: 1 teaspoon
Rose water: 1 tablespoon
Oil for frying

Recipe

1. Make Dough: Mix khoya and flour, knead into a smooth dough.
2. Shape: Roll dough into small balls.
3. Fry: Deep fry the balls until golden brown.
4. Soak in Syrup: Dip fried balls in sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water.
5. Serve: Serve warm or chilled.

Rasmalai

Ingredients

Paneer (cottage cheese balls): 10
Milk: 1 liter
Sugar: 1 cup
Saffron: A few strands
Cardamom powder: 1 teaspoon

Recipe

1. Prepare Milk Syrup: Boil milk, add saffron, sugar, and cardamom powder.
2. Add Paneer: Gently add paneer balls and let them soak in the syrup.
3. Simmer: Let it simmer for a few minutes, then cool.
4. Serve: Serve chilled, garnished with nuts.

Jalebi

Ingredients

All-purpose flour: 1 cup
Yogurt: ½ cup
Sugar: 1 cup
Saffron: A few strands
Ghee or oil for frying

Recipe

1. Make Batter: Mix flour and yogurt to form a thick batter, ferment overnight.
2. Prepare Syrup: Make a sugar syrup flavored with saffron.
3. Shape Jalebis: Pipe the batter into hot oil in spiral shapes and fry until crispy.
4. Soak in Syrup: Dip the fried jalebis in syrup and serve hot.
