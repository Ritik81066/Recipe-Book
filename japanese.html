Tempura

Ingredients

Shrimp or Vegetables (sliced): 200g
Tempura Batter Mix: 1 cup
Ice-cold Water: 1 cup
Oil for Deep Frying
Dipping Sauce (Tentsuyu): ½ cup

Recipe

1. Prepare Batter: Mix tempura batter with ice-cold water until smooth.
2. Dip: Coat shrimp or veggies in the batter.
3. Fry: Deep fry in hot oil until golden and crisp.
4. Serve: Serve hot with dipping sauce.

Edamame

Ingredients

Fresh Edamame (soybeans in pods): 2 cups
Sea Salt: 1 tablespoon
Water: For boiling

Recipe

1. Boil: Bring water to a boil and add edamame. Cook for 5-6 minutes.
2. Season: Drain and sprinkle with sea salt.
3. Serve: Serve warm as a snack or appetizer.

Gyoza

Ingredients

Gyoza Wrappers: 20
Ground Pork: 200g
Cabbage (minced): 1 cup
Soy Sauce: 1 tablespoon
Sesame Oil: 1 tablespoon
Garlic (minced): 1 clove
Dipping Sauce: Soy sauce and vinegar

Recipe

1. Prepare Filling: Mix pork, cabbage, garlic, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
2. Wrap: Place filling in gyoza wrappers and fold.
3. Cook: Pan-fry until golden, then steam.
4. Serve: Serve with dipping sauce.

Agedashi Tofu

Ingredients

Firm Tofu: 200g
Potato Starch: ¼ cup
Oil for Frying
Dashi Broth: 1 cup
Soy Sauce: 1 tablespoon
Green Onions (chopped): For garnish

Recipe

1. Prepare Tofu: Coat tofu cubes in potato starch.
2. Fry: Deep fry tofu until crispy.
3. Serve: Serve tofu in dashi broth with soy sauce and garnish with green onions.

Sushi

Ingredients

Sushi Rice: 1 cup
Nori Sheets: 4
Fresh Fish (tuna/salmon): 100g
Soy Sauce: For dipping
Wasabi: 1 tablespoon
Pickled Ginger: For garnish

Recipe

1. Prepare Rice: Cook sushi rice and season with vinegar.
2. Roll: Place rice on nori sheet and add fish. Roll tightly.
3. Serve: Slice rolls and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and ginger.

Ramen

Ingredients

Ramen Noodles: 1 packet
Chicken Broth: 2 cups
Soy Sauce: 1 tablespoon
Boiled Egg: 1
Green Onions (chopped): For garnish
Pork Slices: 100g

Recipe

1. Prepare Broth: Simmer chicken broth with soy sauce.
2. Cook Noodles: Cook ramen noodles in boiling water.
3. Assemble: Pour broth over noodles, add pork slices, egg, and garnish with green onions.

Katsu Curry

Ingredients

Chicken Breast: 1
Panko Breadcrumbs: ½ cup
Curry Sauce: 1 cup
Rice: 1 cup
Flour: ¼ cup
Egg: 1 (beaten)

Recipe

1. Bread Chicken: Coat chicken in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs.
2. Fry: Deep fry until golden brown.
3. Serve: Serve with rice and pour curry sauce over the chicken.

Okonomiyaki

Ingredients

Cabbage (shredded): 1 cup
Flour: ½ cup
Egg: 1
Dashi Broth: ¼ cup
Pork Belly: 100g
Okonomiyaki Sauce: 2 tablespoons
Mayonnaise: For garnish

Recipe

1. Prepare Batter: Mix flour, egg, and dashi broth. Add shredded cabbage.
2. Cook: Pour batter on a hot griddle, top with pork, and cook until golden.
3. Serve: Drizzle with okonomiyaki sauce and mayonnaise before serving.

Mochi

Ingredients

Glutinous Rice Flour: 1 cup
Water: ¾ cup
Sugar: ¼ cup
Cornstarch: For dusting
Red Bean Paste (optional): For filling

Recipe

1. Prepare Dough: Mix rice flour, water, and sugar. Steam until doughy.
2. Shape: Dust hands with cornstarch, shape mochi into balls, and fill with red bean paste if desired.
3. Serve: Serve fresh or chilled.

Dorayaki

Ingredients

Eggs: 2
Sugar: ½ cup
Flour: 1 cup
Baking Powder: 1 teaspoon
Red Bean Paste: ½ cup

Recipe

1. Prepare Batter: Mix eggs, sugar, flour, and baking powder.
2. Cook: Pour batter on a griddle, cook until golden on both sides.
3. Assemble: Sandwich red bean paste between two pancakes and serve.

Taiyaki

Ingredients

Pancake Batter: 1 cup
Red Bean Paste: ½ cup
Oil: For greasing

Recipe

1. Prepare Mold: Heat a taiyaki mold and grease with oil.
2. Fill: Pour pancake batter into the mold, add red bean paste, and cover with more batter.
3. Cook: Cook until golden and crisp. Serve hot.

Matcha Ice Cream

Ingredients

Heavy Cream: 1 cup
Milk: 1 cup
Sugar: ½ cup
Matcha Powder: 1 tablespoon

Recipe

1. Mix Ingredients: Whisk together cream, milk, sugar, and matcha powder.
2. Chill: Pour into an ice cream maker and churn until thick. Freeze until firm.
3. Serve: Serve scoops of matcha ice cream in bowls or cones.
