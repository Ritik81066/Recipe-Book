Quesadilla

Ingredients

Tortillas: 2
Cheese (shredded): 1 cup
Optional fillings: Chicken, beans, veggies
Salsa and sour cream for serving

Recipe

1. Heat: Heat a tortilla in a pan.
2. Add cheese: Sprinkle cheese and other fillings on one half.
3. Fold & Cook: Fold the tortilla and cook until crispy and golden.
4. Serve: Serve with salsa and sour cream.

Guacamole

Ingredients

Ripe avocados: 2
Onion (finely chopped): ½ cup
Tomato (diced): ½ cup
Lime juice: 1 tablespoon
Salt and pepper to taste
Cilantro for garnish

Recipe

1. Mash avocados: In a bowl, mash the avocados.
2. Add ingredients: Stir in onion, tomato, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
3. Mix: Mix everything together and garnish with cilantro.
4. Serve: Serve with tortilla chips.

Chicken Taquitos

Ingredients

Shredded chicken: 1 cup
Tortillas: 10
Cheese (optional): ½ cup
Salsa for serving
Oil for frying

Recipe

1. Fill: Place shredded chicken on each tortilla. Add cheese if desired.
2. Roll: Roll the tortillas tightly and secure with toothpicks.
3. Fry: Deep fry until crispy and golden brown.
4. Serve: Serve with salsa.

Elote

Ingredients

Corn on the cob: 4
Mayonnaise: ¼ cup
Cotija cheese (crumbled): ½ cup
Chili powder: 1 teaspoon
Lime wedges for serving

Recipe

1. Grill corn: Grill the corn until slightly charred.
2. Coat: Spread mayonnaise on the corn.
3. Top: Sprinkle with Cotija cheese and chili powder.
4. Serve: Serve with lime wedges.

Tacos al Pastor

Ingredients

Pork (thinly sliced): 1 lb
Pineapple (diced): ½ cup
Tortillas: 10
Onion (chopped): ½ cup
Cilantro: ¼ cup
Lime wedges for serving

Recipe

1. Cook pork: Cook the pork until well browned.
2. Assemble tacos: Place pork, pineapple, onion, and cilantro in each tortilla.
3. Serve: Serve with lime wedges.

Chiles en Nogada

Ingredients

Poblano peppers: 4
Ground meat (beef or pork): 1 lb
Raisins: ¼ cup
Walnuts (chopped): ¼ cup
Nogada sauce: 1 cup
Pomegranate seeds for garnish

Recipe

1. Roast peppers: Roast and peel poblano peppers.
2. Prepare filling: Cook ground meat with raisins and walnuts.
3. Fill peppers: Stuff the peppers with the meat mixture.
4. Top: Cover with nogada sauce and garnish with pomegranate seeds.

Enchiladas

Ingredients

Tortillas: 10
Shredded chicken or beef: 1 lb
Enchilada sauce: 1 cup
Cheese (shredded): 1 cup
Sour cream for serving

Recipe

1. Fill tortillas: Add meat to each tortilla.
2. Roll & place: Roll the tortillas and place them in a baking dish.
3. Top with sauce: Pour enchilada sauce and sprinkle with cheese.
4. Bake: Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
5. Serve: Serve with sour cream.

Vegetarian Burrito

Ingredients

Tortillas: 2
Rice: 1 cup
Black beans: ½ cup
Avocado (sliced): 1
Salsa: ½ cup
Lettuce: ½ cup

Recipe

1. Layer ingredients: Add rice, beans, avocado, salsa, and lettuce to each tortilla.
2. Roll: Roll the tortillas tightly.
3. Serve: Serve with additional salsa or hot sauce.

Churros

Ingredients

Flour: 1 cup
Water: 1 cup
Butter: 2 tablespoons
Sugar: ¼ cup
Cinnamon: 1 teaspoon
Oil for frying

Recipe

1. Make dough: Boil water, butter, and flour to form a dough.
2. Shape: Pipe the dough into hot oil using a star-shaped nozzle.
3. Fry: Fry until golden and crispy.
4. Coat: Roll in sugar and cinnamon while hot.
5. Serve: Serve with chocolate sauce.

Flan

Ingredients

Eggs: 4
Sweetened condensed milk: 1 can
Evaporated milk: 1 can
Sugar: 1 cup (for caramel)
Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon

Recipe

1. Make caramel: Heat sugar until melted and golden, then pour into a mold.
2. Mix ingredients: Blend eggs, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla.
3. Bake: Pour the mixture over caramel and bake in a water bath until set.
4. Serve: Chill and invert to serve.

Tres Leches Cake

Ingredients

Flour: 1 cup
Eggs: 4
Sugar: 1 cup
Whole milk: 1/3 cup
Evaporated milk: 1 can
Sweetened condensed milk: 1 can
Heavy cream: 1 cup

Recipe

1. Bake cake: Mix flour, eggs, and sugar to make the cake. Bake until golden.
2. Make milk mixture: Combine evaporated milk, condensed milk, and cream.
3. Soak cake: Poke holes in the cake and pour the milk mixture over it.
4. Chill & serve: Chill and serve with whipped cream.

Cajeta

Ingredients

Goat’s milk: 4 cups
Sugar: 1 cup
Cinnamon: 1 stick
Baking soda: 1/4 teaspoon

Recipe

1. Boil milk: Heat milk with sugar and cinnamon until simmering.
2. Thicken: Add baking soda and stir constantly as the mixture thickens.
3. Cook: Cook until it becomes a thick caramel.
4. Cool & serve: Let it cool and serve as a topping for desserts.
