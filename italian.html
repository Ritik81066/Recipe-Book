Bruschetta

Ingredients

Baguette: 1
Tomatoes (diced): 2 cups
Fresh basil (chopped): ½ cup
Olive oil: 3 tablespoons
Garlic (minced): 2 cloves
Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Prepare Topping: Mix tomatoes, basil, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
2. Toast Bread: Slice baguette, toast until golden.
3. Assemble: Top toasted bread with tomato mixture.
4. Serve: Serve immediately as an appetizer.

Caprese Salad

Ingredients

Fresh mozzarella: 250g
Tomatoes (sliced): 2 large
Fresh basil leaves: 1 bunch
Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
Balsamic vinegar: 1 tablespoon
Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Layer: Alternate layers of mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil.
2. Dress: Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
3. Season: Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Serve: Serve immediately as a refreshing salad.

Calamari

Ingredients

Fresh calamari (cleaned and sliced): 500g
Flour: 1 cup
Cornstarch: ½ cup
Salt and pepper to taste
Oil for frying
Lemon wedges for serving

Recipe

1. Mix Breading: Combine flour, cornstarch, salt, and pepper.
2. Coat Calamari: Dredge calamari in the mixture.
3. Fry: Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown.
4. Serve: Serve hot with lemon wedges.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients

Button mushrooms: 12
Cream cheese: 200g
Garlic (minced): 2 cloves
Parmesan cheese (grated): 50g
Fresh parsley (chopped): 2 tablespoons
Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Preheat Oven: Preheat to 180°C (350°F).
2. Prepare Filling: Mix cream cheese, garlic, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper.
3. Stuff Mushrooms: Fill each mushroom cap with the mixture.
4. Bake: Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden.

Bolognese

Ingredients

Ground beef: 500g
Onion (chopped): 1
Carrots (chopped): 1
Celery (chopped): 1 stalk
Tomato sauce: 400g
Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Sauté Veggies: Heat oil and sauté onion, carrots, and celery until soft.
2. Add Meat: Add ground beef and cook until browned.
3. Simmer: Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper; simmer for 30 minutes.
4. Serve: Serve over pasta.

Pesto Pasta

Ingredients

Pasta (your choice): 250g
Fresh basil: 2 cups
Pine nuts: ¼ cup
Olive oil: ½ cup
Parmesan cheese (grated): 50g
Garlic (minced): 2 cloves
Salt to taste

Recipe

1. Cook Pasta: Cook pasta according to package instructions.
2. Make Pesto: Blend basil, pine nuts, olive oil, Parmesan, garlic, and salt until smooth.
3. Toss: Toss cooked pasta with pesto.
4. Serve: Serve immediately, garnished with more Parmesan.

Tiramisu

Ingredients

Mascarpone cheese: 250g
Coffee (strong brewed): 1 cup
Ladyfinger cookies: 200g
Cocoa powder: for dusting
Sugar: 100g
Eggs: 2

Recipe

1. Prepare Mixture: Whisk egg yolks and sugar, then fold in mascarpone.
2. Dip Cookies: Quickly dip ladyfingers in coffee.
3. Layer: Layer cookies and mascarpone mixture in a dish.
4. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, dust with cocoa before serving.

Panna Cotta

Ingredients

Heavy cream: 500ml
Sugar: 100g
Gelatin: 2 teaspoons
Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon
Fresh berries for garnish

Recipe

1. Heat Cream: In a saucepan, heat cream and sugar until dissolved.
2. Add Gelatin: Dissolve gelatin in cold water, then stir into the cream.
3. Pour: Pour mixture into molds and refrigerate until set.
4. Serve: Serve chilled, garnished with fresh berries.

Cannoli

Ingredients

Cannoli shells: 12
Ricotta cheese: 400g
Powdered sugar: 100g
Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon
Mini chocolate chips: 50g
Chopped pistachios for garnish

Recipe

1. Prepare Filling: Mix ricotta, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
2. Fill Shells: Pipe the filling into cannoli shells.
3. Garnish: Dip ends in chocolate chips and pistachios.
4. Serve: Serve immediately for best texture.

Gelato

Ingredients

Milk: 500ml
Heavy cream: 250ml
Sugar: 100g
Egg yolks: 4
Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon

Recipe

1. Heat Mixture: In a saucepan, heat milk, cream, and half of the sugar until warm.
2. Whisk Yolks: In a bowl, whisk egg yolks with remaining sugar until pale.
3. Combine: Gradually whisk warm mixture into yolks, then return to saucepan.
4. Chill: Cool and churn in an ice cream maker until creamy.

Lasagna

Ingredients

Lasagna noodles: 12 sheets
Ground beef: 500g
Tomato sauce: 700g
Ricotta cheese: 400g
Mozzarella cheese (shredded): 200g
Parmesan cheese (grated): 100g
Onion (chopped): 1
Garlic (minced): 2 cloves
Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Preheat Oven: Preheat to 180°C (350°F).
2. Sauté Beef: In a pan, heat olive oil, sauté onion and garlic, then add ground beef; cook until browned.
3. Layer: In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers. Top with Parmesan.
4. Bake: Bake for 30-35 minutes until cheese is bubbly. Let cool before slicing.

Parmigiana

Ingredients

Eggplant (sliced): 2 large
Tomato sauce: 500g
Mozzarella cheese (sliced): 200g
Parmesan cheese (grated): 100g
Olive oil: for frying
Fresh basil leaves: for garnish
Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Prepare Eggplant: Salt eggplant slices and let sit for 30 minutes to draw out moisture. Rinse and pat dry.
2. Fry: Heat olive oil in a pan and fry eggplant slices until golden brown.
3. Layer: In a baking dish, layer eggplant, tomato sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat layers.
4. Bake: Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25-30 minutes until bubbly. Garnish with basil before serving.
