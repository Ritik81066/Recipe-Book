Spring Rolls

Ingredients

Spring Roll Wrappers: 10-12
Cabbage (shredded): 1 cup
Carrots (julienned): ½ cup
Capsicum (julienned): ½ cup
Soy sauce: 1 tablespoon
Salt and pepper to taste
Oil for deep frying

Recipe

1. Prepare Filling: Stir-fry veggies in a pan with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
2. Wrap: Place the filling in the center of each wrapper and roll tightly.
3. Fry: Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown.
4. Serve: Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

Dumplings

Ingredients

Dumpling Wrappers: 20
Ground Pork: 1 cup
Cabbage (finely chopped): 1 cup
Green Onions: 2 (chopped)
Ginger (minced): 1 teaspoon
Soy Sauce: 2 tablespoons
Sesame Oil: 1 tablespoon
Salt and Pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Make Filling: Mix all filling ingredients in a bowl.
2. Wrap: Place a spoonful of filling on each wrapper and fold.
3. Cook: Steam or boil until cooked through.
4. Serve: Serve with soy sauce for dipping.

Chicken Wings

Ingredients

Chicken Wings: 1 kg
Soy Sauce: ½ cup
Honey: ¼ cup
Garlic (minced): 2 cloves
Ginger (grated): 1 tablespoon
Sesame Seeds: for garnish

Recipe

1. Marinate: Combine soy sauce, honey, garlic, and ginger. Marinate wings for at least 30 minutes.
2. Bake: Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 30-35 minutes, turning halfway.
3. Garnish: Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.

Vegetable Spring Rolls

Ingredients

Spring Roll Wrappers: 10-12
Cabbage (shredded): 1 cup
Carrots (julienned): ½ cup
Bell Peppers (julienned): ½ cup
Soy Sauce: 1 tablespoon
Salt and Pepper to taste
Oil for frying

Recipe

1. Prepare Filling: Stir-fry veggies with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
2. Wrap: Place filling on wrappers and roll tightly.
3. Fry: Deep fry until golden brown.
4. Serve: Serve with sweet chili sauce.

Fried Rice

Ingredients

Cooked Rice: 3 cups
Mixed Vegetables: 1 cup
Soy Sauce: 3 tablespoons
Eggs: 2 (beaten)
Green Onions: 2 (chopped)
Oil: 2 tablespoons
Salt and Pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Heat Oil: In a pan, heat oil and scramble the eggs.
2. Add Veggies: Add mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
3. Add Rice: Add cooked rice, soy sauce, and mix well.
4. Serve: Garnish with green onions before serving.

Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken Breast: 500g (cubed)
Peanuts: ½ cup
Bell Peppers: 1 (diced)
Soy Sauce: 3 tablespoons
Rice Vinegar: 1 tablespoon
Garlic (minced): 2 cloves
Ginger (grated): 1 tablespoon
Red Pepper Flakes: to taste
Cornstarch: 1 tablespoon (optional for thickening)

Recipe

1. Marinate Chicken: Combine chicken with soy sauce and let sit for 15 minutes.
2. Cook Chicken: Stir-fry in a hot pan until cooked through.
3. Add Veggies: Add peppers, peanuts, and spices. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
4. Serve: Serve hot with rice.

Beef and Broccoli

Ingredients

Beef (sliced thin): 500g
Broccoli: 2 cups (cut into florets)
Soy Sauce: 3 tablespoons
Oyster Sauce: 2 tablespoons
Garlic (minced): 2 cloves
Ginger (grated): 1 tablespoon
Oil for stir-frying

Recipe

1. Marinate Beef: Combine beef with soy sauce and let sit for 15 minutes.
2. Stir-Fry: Heat oil and stir-fry beef until browned. Remove and set aside.
3. Add Broccoli: Stir-fry broccoli until tender, then add beef back in.
4. Serve: Serve hot with rice.

Mapo Tofu

Ingredients

Firm Tofu: 400g (cubed)
Ground Pork: 200g
Doubanjiang (spicy bean paste): 1 tablespoon
Soy Sauce: 2 tablespoons
Garlic (minced): 2 cloves
Ginger (grated): 1 tablespoon
Green Onions: 2 (chopped)
Sichuan Peppercorns: to taste
Oil for cooking

Recipe

1. Cook Pork: Heat oil and stir-fry ground pork until browned.
2. Add Tofu: Carefully add tofu and cook with doubanjiang and soy sauce.
3. Simmer: Let simmer until heated through.
4. Serve: Garnish with green onions and peppercorns.

Mango Pudding

Ingredients

Ripe Mangoes: 2 (pureed)
Coconut Milk: 1 cup
Sugar: ¼ cup
Gelatin: 2 tablespoons (dissolved in water)
Water: ½ cup

Recipe

1. Blend: Blend mangoes, coconut milk, and sugar until smooth.
2. Add Gelatin: Stir in dissolved gelatin and mix well.
3. Chill: Pour into molds and refrigerate until set.
4. Serve: Serve chilled as a dessert.

Sesame Balls

Ingredients

Glutinous Rice Flour: 2 cups
Water: ¾ cup
Red Bean Paste: 1 cup
Sesame Seeds: ½ cup
Oil for frying

Recipe

1. Make Dough: Mix glutinous rice flour and water to form a dough.
2. Form Balls: Take a piece of dough, fill with red bean paste, and roll into a ball.
3. Coat: Roll in sesame seeds.
4. Fry: Deep fry until golden brown and serve warm.

Egg Tarts

Ingredients

Tart Shells: 10
Eggs: 3
Sugar: ½ cup
Milk: 1 cup
Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon

Recipe

1. Preheat Oven: Preheat to 180°C (350°F).
2. Mix Filling: Whisk eggs, sugar, milk, and vanilla until smooth.
3. Fill Shells: Pour mixture into tart shells.
4. Bake: Bake for 25-30 minutes or until set.

Red Bean Soup

Ingredients

Red Beans: 1 cup (soaked overnight)
Water: 4 cups
Sugar: ½ cup (to taste)
Coconut Milk: optional for serving

Recipe

1. Cook Beans: In a pot, boil red beans in water until soft (about 1-2 hours).
2. Add Sugar: Stir in sugar until dissolved.
3. Serve: Serve warm, drizzled with coconut milk if desired.
