French Onion Soup

Ingredients

Onions (thinly sliced): 4 large
Butter: 2 tablespoons
Beef broth: 4 cups
White wine: ½ cup
Baguette slices (toasted): 8
Gruyère cheese (grated): 1 cup
Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Caramelize Onions: Sauté onions in butter until golden and caramelized.
2. Add Broth: Stir in beef broth and white wine, and simmer for 20 minutes.
3. Prepare Bowls: Ladle soup into bowls, place toasted baguette slices on top, and cover with grated Gruyère.
4. Broil: Broil until cheese is bubbly and golden. Serve hot.

Escargots

Ingredients

Snails (canned or fresh): 12
Garlic (minced): 2 cloves
Parsley (chopped): 2 tablespoons
Butter: 4 tablespoons
White wine: ¼ cup
Salt and pepper to taste
Baguette slices for serving

Recipe

1. Prepare Butter Sauce: Melt butter in a pan, add garlic and parsley, and sauté for a minute.
2. Add Snails: Stir in snails and white wine, cooking until heated through.
3. Serve: Serve hot with baguette slices to soak up the sauce.

Quiche Lorraine

Ingredients

Pie crust: 1 (store-bought or homemade)
Bacon (chopped): 8 slices
Eggs: 4
Heavy cream: 1 cup
Gruyère cheese (grated): 1 cup
Salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste

Recipe

1. Cook Bacon: Fry bacon until crispy, then drain.
2. Prepare Filling: Whisk eggs, cream, salt, pepper, and nutmeg together.
3. Assemble Quiche: Spread bacon and cheese in the pie crust, then pour the egg mixture over.
4. Bake: Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes until set. Serve warm.

Ratatouille

Ingredients

Eggplant (sliced): 1
Zucchini (sliced): 1
Tomatoes (sliced): 3
Bell peppers (sliced): 2
Onion (sliced): 1
Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
Herbs de Provence: 1 tablespoon
Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Sauté Vegetables: Sauté onions, bell peppers, and tomatoes in olive oil.
2. Layer: In a baking dish, layer eggplant, zucchini, and sautéed vegetables.
3. Bake: Drizzle with olive oil and herbs, bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-35 minutes.
4. Serve: Serve hot as a main or side dish.

Coq au Vin

Ingredients

Chicken (cut into pieces): 1
Red wine: 2 cups
Bacon (chopped): 4 slices
Mushrooms (sliced): 1 cup
Garlic (minced): 3 cloves
Onion (chopped): 1
Thyme: 1 tablespoon
Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Marinate Chicken: Marinate chicken in red wine, garlic, and thyme for 2 hours.
2. Sauté Bacon and Veggies: Cook bacon, then sauté onions, garlic, and mushrooms.
3. Cook Chicken: Brown chicken, then add marinade and simmer for 45 minutes.
4. Serve: Serve hot with mashed potatoes or crusty bread.

Boeuf Bourguignon

Ingredients

Beef stew meat: 2 lbs
Red wine: 2 cups
Bacon (chopped): 4 slices
Carrots (sliced): 2
Mushrooms (sliced): 1 cup
Onion (chopped): 1
Garlic (minced): 3 cloves
Thyme: 1 tablespoon
Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

1. Brown Beef: Brown the beef in a pan, then set aside.
2. Sauté Veggies: Cook bacon, then sauté onions, garlic, carrots, and mushrooms.
3. Simmer: Add beef, wine, and thyme, simmer for 2-3 hours until tender.
4. Serve: Serve with mashed potatoes or crusty bread.

Bouillabaisse

Ingredients

Fish fillets (white fish, cod, or sea bass): 1 lb
Shrimp: ½ lb
Mussels: ½ lb
Onion (chopped): 1
Garlic (minced): 3 cloves
Tomatoes (chopped): 2
Fennel: 1 bulb (sliced)
Saffron: a pinch
Fish stock: 4 cups

Recipe

1. Sauté Aromatics: Sauté onion, garlic, and fennel in olive oil.
2. Add Stock and Saffron: Pour in fish stock, add saffron and tomatoes, simmer for 15 minutes.
3. Add Seafood: Add fish, shrimp, and mussels, cook until seafood is done (5-7 minutes).
4. Serve: Serve hot with crusty bread and rouille sauce.

Crème Brûlée

Ingredients

Heavy cream: 2 cups
Vanilla bean: 1
Egg yolks: 5
Sugar: ½ cup
Extra sugar for caramelizing: 2 tablespoons

Recipe

1. Heat Cream: Heat cream and vanilla bean in a saucepan until steaming, then remove from heat.
2. Mix Yolks and Sugar: Whisk egg yolks and sugar, then slowly pour in the cream.
3. Bake: Pour mixture into ramekins and bake in a water bath at 325°F (160°C) for 40 minutes.
4. Caramelize Sugar: Chill, then sprinkle sugar on top and torch until caramelized.

Tarte Tatin

Ingredients

Apples (peeled and sliced): 6
Puff pastry: 1 sheet
Sugar: ½ cup
Butter: 4 tablespoons
Lemon juice: 1 tablespoon

Recipe

1. Cook Apples: Cook apples with sugar, butter, and lemon juice until caramelized.
2. Prepare Pastry: Place apples in a baking dish, cover with puff pastry.
3. Bake: Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes until golden.
4. Serve: Invert onto a plate and serve warm.

Chocolate Soufflé

Ingredients

Dark chocolate (chopped): 6 oz
Butter: 2 tablespoons
Eggs (separated): 4
Sugar: ¼ cup
Cream of tartar: ¼ teaspoon

Recipe

1. Melt Chocolate: Melt chocolate and butter in a double boiler.
2. Whisk Yolks and Sugar: Beat egg yolks and sugar, then mix in chocolate.
3. Whisk Whites: Beat egg whites with cream of tartar until stiff peaks form.
4. Bake: Gently fold whites into chocolate mixture and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 12-15 minutes.

Madeleines

Ingredients

Butter (melted): ½ cup
Sugar: ⅔ cup
Eggs: 2
Flour: 1 cup
Baking powder: 1 teaspoon
Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon

Recipe

1. Whisk Eggs and Sugar: Beat eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.
2. Mix in Flour and Butter: Fold in flour, baking powder, and melted butter.
3. Bake: Spoon batter into a madeleine mold and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 10-12 minutes.
4. Serve: Dust with powdered sugar and serve.
